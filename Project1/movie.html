The Shawshank Redemption

The Shawshank Redemption is my all-time favorite movie. It tells the story of a banker who is wrongly convicted of murder and sent to prison, where he befriends a fellow inmate and manages to find hope and redemption in a place where most would give up. The movie is a masterpiece of storytelling and acting, and it never fails to move me every time I watch it.
